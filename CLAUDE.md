# Client Pulse

## What
Local web dashboard for managing my online calisthenics coaching clients.

## Why
Replacing Google Sheets — it breaks when clients pause, can't clear renewal flags, and has no visual timeline.

## Key files
- `schema.json` — single source of truth for client data structure
- `clients_seed.json` — real client data (40 clients from my spreadsheet)
- `planning_context.md` — business rules and decisions
